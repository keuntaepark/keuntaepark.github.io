Key Outcomes

  • Created a reusable segmentation pipeline applicable to other datasets or industries.

Reflection

  • Challenge: Frequency-only models were too shallow to capture meaningful patterns.
  • Approach: Searched literature and experimented with Interpurchase Time; adjusted the formula to fit the dataset and validated performance through clustering quality metrics.
  • Takeaway: Learned how to design features that better represent real-world behavior and integrate them into practical business workflows.
