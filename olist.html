Problem

An initial analysis of the Olist e-commerce platform revealed a critical customer churn issue, with a repeat purchase rate of less than 4%. This low retention rate hinders LTV (Customer Lifetime Value) growth and forces a heavy reliance on new customer acquisition, threatening the long-term sustainability of the business. This project aimed to identify the root cause of churn and propose a data-driven, verifiable solution.

Action

  • Problem Quantification & Pattern Discovery (EDA & Cohort Analysis):
    • Pinpointed the primary leakage point via AARRR funnel analysis, which showed a 96% drop-off rate between the Activation and Retention stages.
    • Visually proved through Cohort Analysis that this high initial churn was a long-standing, structural problem, not a one-time issue.

      • Figure 01: Monthly Retention Rate Heatmap

  • Root Cause Analysis (LTV & Regional Analysis):
    • Hypothesized that a poor first-purchase experience, driven by delivery performance, was the root cause of churn.
    • LTV analysis revealed that 53.5% of total revenue comes from the top 20% of VIP customers, who are heavily concentrated in well-serviced southeastern metropolitan areas (e.g., SP, RJ).
    • In contrast, regions with the highest rate of "bad delivery experiences" (>16 days) were concentrated in the North/Northeast, proving a strong correlation between delivery experience, LTV, and churn.

      • Figure 02: LTV Distribution and Box Plot

  • Solution Design & Impact Simulation (ML & A/B Test):
    • Designed a solution to mitigate the negative impact of poor delivery: proactively offering compensation to affected customers.
    • Developed a repurchase prediction ML model, comparing multiple algorithms (Logistic Regression, RandomForest, XGBoost) and applying advanced techniques like SMOTE, Cost-Sensitive Learning, and GridSearchCV to handle severe class imbalance.
    • An A/B test simulation using the champion model predicted that offering a 10% discount coupon to customers with delayed deliveries would increase their repurchase probability by 2.58 percentage points, yielding a positive ROI of approximately 38.8%.

Result

  • Identified Key Driver of Churn: Proved with data that inconsistent and poor delivery experiences are a primary cause of Olist's low customer retention.
  • Proposed a Data-Driven, Actionable Solution: Moved beyond insights to propose a concrete action planâ€”a 10% coupon for delayed ordersâ€”backed by a clear expected ROI of 38.8%, providing a strong business case for implementation.
  • Informed Strategic Decision-Making: Provided a clear, data-driven direction for the business: instead of broad marketing efforts, retention resources should be focused on targeted interventions for specific customer segments who have had a poor delivery experience.

Tech Stack

  • Language: Python
  • Data Processing & Analysis: Pandas, NumPy, SQL (via `sqlite3`)
  • Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Machine Learning: Scikit-learn (Logistic Regression, RandomForest, StandardScaler, GridSearchCV), XGBoost
  • Imbalanced Data Handling: imbalanced-learn (SMOTE)
